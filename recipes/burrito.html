Burrito

Description

All the mexican ingredients rolled up into a tortilla.

Ingredients

  • Beans
  • Cheese
  • Al Pastor (duh)
  • Salsa
  • Guac
  • Jalepenos
  • Sour Cream
  • Tortillas

Steps

  1. Cook the meat
  2. Warm the beans
  3. Warm the tortillas
  4. Roll every ingredient inside tortillas
